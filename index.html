About Tairac Digital Solution

Tairac Digital Solution is a top-tier service provider helping individuals, businesses, and NGOs register legally with Nigeria’s Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC). Whether you're a student, entrepreneur, or church, we guide you step-by-step through seamless registration.

Our Services

  • Business Name Registration
  • Limited Liability Company (LLC) Registration
  • NGO and Incorporated Trustees Registration
  • Annual Returns and CAC Compliance
  • Name Search & Reservation
  • Post-Incorporation Filings
  • Advisory & Legal Support

Trusted CAC Partner

Our CAC specialists work round the clock to ensure you get the best service with zero stress.

Why Register with CAC?

Registering with CAC gives your business legitimacy, access to financial institutions, brand protection, trust from customers, and eligibility for grants, contracts, and investment opportunities. Our team ensures you're registered legally and efficiently.

Our Process is Simple

  • Click Get Started
  • Choose your CAC registration type
  • Select your package (Silver, Gold, or Diamond)
  • Click on Apply now to fill the requirements
  • Click on the link below the requirements form to proceed to payment page
  • Pay securely via Paystack
  • Track progress & get confirmation via WhatsApp
