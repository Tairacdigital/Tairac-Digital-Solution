About CAC (Corporate Affairs Commission)

The Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) is a federal government agency established under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) in Nigeria. It is responsible for the regulation and supervision of the formation, incorporation, registration, and management of companies and businesses in Nigeria.

The CAC ensures that all businesses operating in Nigeria comply with the legal framework that governs them. Whether you are running a sole proprietorship, a limited liability company, or a non-profit organization, CAC registration is your first step toward legitimacy and growth.
