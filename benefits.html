Benefits of CAC Registration

  • Legal Recognition: It gives your business or NGO legal status in Nigeria.
  • Business Bank Account: Use your CAC certificate to open a corporate bank account.
  • Brand Protection: Prevent others from using your business name.
  • Access to Loans: CAC registration is required to apply for many SME loans and grants.
  • Credibility: Customers and partners trust legally registered businesses more.
  • Contract Eligibility: Many organizations and government agencies only work with registered entities.
  • Grants & Funding: International and local funders require CAC documentation to give support.
  • Online Presence: Platforms like Paystack, Flutterwave, and Google Business require CAC documents.
