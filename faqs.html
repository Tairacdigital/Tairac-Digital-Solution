CAC (Corporate Affairs Commission) is the government agency responsible for registering businesses, companies, and NGOs in Nigeria.

Business Name registration usually takes 3–5 working days. Company or NGO registration may take 7–14 days depending on document readiness.

For Business Name: Full name, passport photo, business name ideas, and address. For Company/NGO: ID cards, passport photos, business objectives, trustee/director details.
